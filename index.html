About Me

Hi Class! My name is John Mish, I was born in Philadelphia, I currently live in Newtown, Bucks County with my wife Madison and our bulldog Tank. I have worked in Real Estate for the past 3+ years, specifically residential sales & property management.

I graduated from New York University in 2009. I also played college basketball while at NYU. Upon graduating, I worked for several companies while living in New York City, most notably Foot Locker, Inc. At Foot Locker, I managed a team of replenishment analysts. Our team was responsible for inventory allocation to over 1,000 Foot Locker locations. After spending nearly ten years in NYC, I moved back home to the Philly area and began working in Real Estate.

In my free time, I enjoy playing basketball, following the NFL (Go Birds!) & NBA (Go Sixers!), as well as traveling. In recent years, I have visted Hong Kong (pictured), Thailand, Italy, Iceland as well as various US Cities, including New Orleans, Denver, Nashville, Chicago and more!

I'm excited to learn more about coding through this class. For the time being, I'm hoping to improve my Real Estate marketing and then eventually pursue a career in tech within the sports industry!
